Ghanian Akan Naming System

In Ghana, the Akan people have inheritedly been naming their children based on the day and even order that they got born. Most Ghanians have at least one name from this kind of system. This namin system is has been shared throuhout West Africa, and even the diaspora. The day names are believed to have deeper meanings , this is associated with the soul and character of someone. While the middle names, have vairious choices such as their birth-order or even twin status or an ancestor's middle name. These Akan names signify the date when the child was born.This organised naming system has been inherited from ancestors . Through this naming system, it is possible to tell the date when one was born and factors leading to their birth.This naming system has attracted many people as it is quite unique.
